Tina Robertson | About

Welcome to my abstract world of artistic exploration! I'm Tina Robertson, an abstract artist renowned for my dynamic compositions and vibrant use of acrylic, spray paint, and Posca pen techniques. Alongside my abstract work, I specialize in captivating human and animal portraits. Drawing inspiration from the chaos of urban life and the tranquility of nature, my pieces are a fusion of bold colors and intricate details, inviting viewers to embark on a visual journey of introspection and interpretation. Join me as we explore the limitless possibilities of abstract expressionism, where each stroke tells a story waiting to be discovered and embraced.
